safiyah riddle

Freelance journalist and masters candidate at Craig Newmark School of Journalism

Safiyah graduated from Haverford College in 2018 with a B.A. in Political Science and a minor in Economics. After graduating, Safiyah taught economics at an international boarding school in Johannesburg, South Africa, where her students represented over 47 countries worldwide. Most recently, Safiyah returned to her hometown of New York City, where she has had roles as an administrative press assistant in the Mayor’s Office, as well as a progressive political communications consulting company. After obtaining her Masters degree from the Craig Newmark School of Journalism at the City University of New York, Safiyah will continue to report on issues most important to New Yorkers.
